City Vehicle Sticker Online Raffle Official Rules

1. SPONSOR: City Vehicle Sticker online sweepstakes (“Raffle”) is sponsored by the Chicago City Clerk’s Office (“Sponsor”) whose address is 121 N. LaSalle, Room 107, Chicago, Illinois 60602.

2. ELIGIBILITY: NO PURCHASE, PAYMENT OR CONTRIBUTION NECESSARY TO ENTER TO WIN. The raffle is open only to residents of the City of Chicago, who are 18 years of age and older, who own, lease, license, or otherwise have exclusive control of a motor vehicle, and who, if eligible to purchase a 2012-2013 Wheel Tax License Emblem online, did not purchase a 2012-2013 Wheel Tax License Emblem in person at any City of Chicago or authorized agent’s sales location prior to July 15, 2012. Employees of the Chicago City Clerk’s Office, donor organizations and their advertising agencies, consultants, suppliers and family members of any of the above are not eligible to win.

3. ENTRY: All persons purchasing a Wheel Tax License Emblem online between April 23, 2012 at 12:01 a.m. and July 15, 2012 at 11:59 p.m. may elect to be entered in the in the Raffle, for a one (1) prize of the participant’s choice, through an online entry. All other eligible persons may enter in the Raffle, for a one (1) prize of the participant’s choice, by completing an entry form at the Chicago of Chicago Clerk’s main office, 121 N. LaSalle, Room 107, Chicago, Illinois 60602, during regular business hours. Limit one (1) entry per person and per registered vehicle. Completed entry forms must be postmarked by July 16, 2012 and mailed to the Chicago Clerk’s main office, 121 N. LaSalle, Room 107, Chicago, Illinois 60602.
